International Students Enrolment

Author: Mun Hong

Date: 21-09-2023

The main task of this visualization is to compare the number of students enrolled in different countries, analyze the contributing factors to students' decisions to study in a particular country, visualize which Australian state has the highest number of students, analyze the relationship between visa grant rate and the number of enrollments, and finally compare the number of students in Australia and New Zealand.

Comparing number of students enrolled in different countries

The choropleth map shows the number of international student enrollments by countries from 2013 to 2022, with a focus on countries such as the USA, UK, and Australia.

Analyze the contributing factors to students' decision to study in a particular country

Factors affecting students' decisions to select a country for their studies are grouped into categories. Hover over each tile to see the percentage of students who selected that particular factor.

Visualize which Australian state has the highest number of students

The bubble map shows that Victoria and New South Wales have the highest number of student enrollments, while Tasmania has the least.
